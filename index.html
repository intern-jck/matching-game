Rules

Try to find matches for all the cards!

Each card has 4 attributes.

Find three cards whose attributes are either are the same or all different.

Below are examples of each attribute and examples of what makes a match.

Each group of 3 cards form a match.

Color

Red, Green, Blue

Texture

Empty, Shaded, Filled

Amount

One, Two, Three

Shape

Triangle, Square, Circle
