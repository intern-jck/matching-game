RULES:

Each card has 4 attributes:

Color, Texture, Number and Shape

A match is when 3 cards have either the same or differnt type of attribute for each of the 4 types.

Example 1

Same color, texture, and number.

Different shape.

Texture

Empty, Shaded, or Filled

Amount

One, Two or Three

Shape

Triangle, Square or Circle
